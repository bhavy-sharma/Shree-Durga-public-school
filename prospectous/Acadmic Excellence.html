Academic Excellence

Pre-Primary Section (Nursery to UKG)

The early childhood curriculum is based on experiential learning, focusing on sensory development, language acquisition, and moral skills enhancement. The key components include:

  • Language Development:
    Introduction to phonics, storytelling, and basic vocabulary.
  • Numeracy Skills:
    Learning numbers, counting, and basic arithmetic concepts through play-based methods.
  • Cognitive & Moral Skills
    Activities such as puzzles, drawing, and hands-on learning.

Primary Section (Class 1 to 5)

We foundational years emphasize a balance between academics and extracurricular activities, ensuring a solid learning base.

  • Languages:
    English and Hindi with structured grammar, reading comprehension, and writing skills.
  • Mathematics:
    Development of analytical and problem-solving skills through engaging methods.
  • Environmental Studies (EVS):
    Awareness about nature, surroundings, and basic science concepts.
  • Computer Science:
    Introduction to basic programming, digital literacy, and internet safety.
  • Co-curricular Activities:
    Art, music, dance, and drama to enhance self-expression and confidence.

Middle Section (Class 6 to 8)

Our evaluation system ensures continuous growth:

  • Science:
    Introduction to Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with practical applications.
  • Social Science:
    History Civics, Geography, and Economics to develop awareness of society and global issues.
