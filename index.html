About

Shree Durga Public School is an English Medium Co-Education School, committed to providing quality education, discipline, and co-curricular activities that shape a bright future.


We offer a balanced approach to learning, ensuring students excel in academics, sports, cultural activities, and social initiatives.


APS was founded in 1987 by Late Shri RRPS Upadhyaya, Retd. Nayab Tehsildar and social worker, with a view to impart quality education to all economic statuses of society.
