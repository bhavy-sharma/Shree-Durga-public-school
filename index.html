VISION & MISSION

Shree Durga Public School is an attempt to redefine schools and reinvent classrooms of the future. An initiative to groom leaders for the new socio-economic order of the Information Age.

AIMS & OBJECTIVE

Imparting quality education to all economic strata of society without any distinction of caste, color, or creed. Bringing awareness in the young generation about national, international, and social issues.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Academic achievement is commonly measured by examinations or continuous assessments. Individual differences in academic performance are linked to intelligence and personality.

About

Shree Durga Public School an English Medium Co-Education School, committed to providing quality education, discipline, and co-curricular activities that shape a bright future.

Shree Durga Public School was founded in 1987 by Late Shree RRPS Upadhyaya,Retd. Nayab Tehsildar and social worker, with a view to impart quality education to all economic statuses of society.

We offer a balanced approach to learning, ensuring students excel in academics, sports, cultural activities, and social initiatives.
