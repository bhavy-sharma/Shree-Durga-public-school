Shree Durga Public School is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. A CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) primary school is an educational institution that follows the CBSE curriculum, which is one of the most widely recognized educational boards in India. The primary level in CBSE schools typically covers classes 1 to 8. The CBSE curriculum emphasizes holistic development, which includes academic learning, extracurricular activities, and life skills. The syllabus is structured to build a strong foundation in subjects like English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and languages like Hindi or regional languages.
