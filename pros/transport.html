School Transport Rules

  1. Students should sit in their place before the bus starts. They should not stand when the bus moves
  2. Students should have their ID cards on while travelling in the School bus.
  3. Students should look after their belongings.
  4. School bags should be kept on the racks and not on the seats.
  5. Silence should be maintained in the bus.
  6. If on the precise stop nobody is available to receive the student, he/she will be brought back to the school and handed over only to the parents in the school campus.
  7. Students should not put out any part of the body from the window or the door.
  8. Cleanliness should be maintained in the bus.
  9. Any damage made by the students to the bus will be reported to the Principal and the parents have to bear the cost.
  10. The students and staff whose names are listed in the route only will be permitted to enter the bus.
  11. School bus service will be cancelled for the students found in misconduct.
  12. Students should be present on the bus stop five minutes prior to the prescribed time. Bus will not wait for the students.
  13. School buses will not stop other than the designated stops. Parents or guardians will not be permitted to stop the bus at any unauthorized stops.
  14. Parents and guardians are not permitted to get inside the bus under any circumstances.
  15. The above rules are made for safety and safe journey of the students.Students should sit in their place before the bus starts. They should not stand when the bus moves.
