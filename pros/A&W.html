Registration for classes from Nursery onwards will take place in December. Forms are available at the school office.

No child will be admitted to a class unless they qualify for an interaction proving their readiness for that class.

Parents must submit a Xerox copy of the original birth certificate, report card from the last examination, and an original Transfer Certificate from the previous school.

The admission fee is non-refundable even if the child does not attend school.

31st July is the last date for admission for the academic year.

WITHDRAWAL

One month's notice in writing or one month's fee in lieu is required for withdrawing a student from the school.

A change in residence or contact number must be notified to the school office in writing. Date of birth once declared in school shall not be changed.

Issuing of Transfer Certificate: The student will be charged all applicable fees until the withdrawal request is submitted in writing.

About the School

Shree Durga Public School is an English Medium Co-Education School committed to providing a well-rounded education that emphasizes not just academic achievement, but also holistic development including sports and cultural activities. We provide students with an environment that nurtures their skills and prepares them to excel in their future endeavors. Our focus is on academic excellence, personal growth, and community engagement.
