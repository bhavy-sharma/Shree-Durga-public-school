Registration for classes from Nursery onwards will in the month of December. Forms for the same are available in the school office.

No child will be admitted to a particular class unless he/she qualifies for an interaction that proves that he/she is fit for that class.

Parents are requested to submit a Xerox copy of the original date of birth certificate and report card of the Last Examination / Test, an original Transfer Certificate from the school last attended must be submitted. The fee paid at the time of admission is not refundable, even if the child does not attend school for a single day. 31 July will be the last day of admission for the academic year.

WITHDRAWAL

One month’s notice in writing or one month’s fee in lieu is required for withdrawing a student from the school.

A change in Residence or contact number must be notified in writing to the school Office. Date of Birth once declared in school, shall not be changed.

Issuing of Transfer Certificate:-

The student will be charged all the applicable fees for those months till he/she does not apply in writing for his/her withdrawal.
