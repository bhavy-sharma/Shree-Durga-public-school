⬅ Back to Gallery

🌟 Special Entrepreneurship Program in Honor of Lt. Shree Ratan Tata Ji 🚀💡 On the occasion of Lt. Shree Ratan Tata Ji’s anniversary, Shree Durga Public School organized a Special Entrepreneurship Program to inspire young minds with the spirit of innovation and leadership. 🎓🔥 The event featured esteemed guests Manish Gaur & Deepika Gaur, two of Ghaziabad’s top business leaders, who shared their incredible success stories and business insights. 💼📈 Born in Tajpur, these visionary entrepreneurs encouraged students to think big, embrace challenges, and turn ideas into reality. 💡🚀 The session covered business strategies, startup tips, financial literacy, and leadership skills, empowering students to build a brighter future. 💰📊 The program left a lasting impact, motivating young minds to dream, create, and innovate! 🌟👏 A heartfelt thanks to our distinguished guests for their guidance and inspiration! 🙏✨ #FutureEntrepreneurs #Inspiration #RatanTataLegacy .
